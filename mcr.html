Middle Common Room

The Middle Common Room (MCR) is a dedicated space for graduate students to relax, work, and spend time with others. Located in the Clore Graduate Centre, it serves as the informal hub of the graduate community.

The room includes comfortable seating, tables, pool and foosball tables, and kitchen facilities. It's used for both individual study and small group gatherings.

Many of the MCR's social events — including BOPs (Big Organised Parties), welfare brunches, and pizza nights — are held here during term.
