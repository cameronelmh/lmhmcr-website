Sports

Sport is a big part of life at college, and there’s loads to get involved in whether you’re keen to join a team or just want something fun and social to do in your spare time.

We’ve got tennis courts for anyone who fancies a hit, and the MCR runs a friendly five-a-side football game on Monday evenings that’s very relaxed and open to everyone.

You’ll also find plenty of more casual options, like spike ball on the lawn and a welcoming running club where people of all speeds and experience levels join in.

If you’re after something more competitive, there are college teams in football, rugby, and cricket, always looking for fresh faces. Whether you want a bit of exercise, to try something new, or to get properly stuck into sport, there’s a way to get involved.
