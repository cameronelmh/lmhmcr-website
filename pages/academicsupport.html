Academic Support & Resources

College Academic Advisor

All students should have been allocated a college academic advisor. If you have not been assigned one, please contact the Academic Office.

Your advisor can help with academic planning, coursework concerns, exam queries, and any other study-related matters.

Contact the Academic Office

Advisor allocation & general queries:

academic.office@lmh.ox.ac.uk

Registrar / academic administration:

academic.registrar@lmh.ox.ac.uk

Please include your full name, course, and year in any email.

Paid Invigilation Opportunities

LMH runs undergraduate collections in 0th week of each term. Graduate students can apply to invigilate for these exams.


Latest Update:

Hilary term collections have now finished. Keep an eye out for Trinity term opportunities.

Talks & Events at LMH

LMH hosts a wide range of talks, seminars, and guest lectures. Stay tuned via college emails and the MCR Instagram for announcements.
