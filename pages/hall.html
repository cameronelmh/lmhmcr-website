Hall

Food

Located in the Deneke Building, the College Dining Hall serves nutritious and varied meals catering to different dietary requirements Monday–Friday during term.

Breakfast is served from 8–10am and costs around £4–6. Lunch is served from 12–2pm, and dinner from 6–7:30pm (excluding Friday, when due to Formal Hall it runs from 5:15–6:15pm). Both lunch and dinner typically cost £5–7. Brunch is also served every Saturday from 10am–2pm.

Formal Hall

One of the highlights of the week during term time is Formal Hall, held every Friday at 7pm. This elegant three-course meal, served by waitstaff, is the perfect way to wind down and celebrate the end of the week. Bring your own drinks, enjoy great food, and relax with friends in a truly memorable setting — it’s an experience not to be missed! Tickets are £16 for students (Graduate Students receive three for free per term) and £23 for guests (up to two). Please see a sample menu below.

Tickets for Formal Hall are released on UPAY every Saturday beforehand at 9am. Due to (very) high demand, we recommend approaching booking these the same way you would concert tickets (seriously).
