Gym

Alongside various college and MCR sport opportunities, LMH also has a gym equipped with a rowing machine, running machine, and weights.

Membership costs £35 for the entire year and you can sign up via your UPAY account. For more information concerning membership, contact the bursar: bursary@lmh.ox.ac.uk
